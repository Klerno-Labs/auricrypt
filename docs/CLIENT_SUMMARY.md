# Client Summary: Auricrypt Plumbing App

**Prepared for:** Auricrypt
**Version:** 1.0.0

Welcome to your new workforce management application! This tool is designed to save you hours of paperwork every week by digitizing your scheduling, invoicing, and inventory tracking.

## How to Get Started

### 1. Logging In
- Visit your app URL (e.g., `app.auricrypt.com`).
- Click **"Sign in with Google"** (recommended) or enter your email and password.
- **Note:** The first time you log in, you will be registered as the **Owner**.

### 2. Adding Your Staff
1.  Click **Team** in the sidebar.
2.  Click **Add Staff Member**.
3.  Enter their name and email.
4.  Select their role (**Staff** or **Manager**).
5.  They will receive an email invite to set up their account.

### 3. Scheduling a Job
1.  Go to the **Calendar** tab.
2.  Click on the date and time you want to schedule the job.
3.  Fill in:
    -   **Customer Name**
    -   **Address**
    -   **Job Description**
    -   **Assign to:** (Select a plumber)
4.  Click **Save Job**.
    *   *Note:* This will automatically appear on the plumber's phone/tablet.

### 4. Creating an Invoice & Getting Paid
This is the fastest way to get paid.

1.  Go to **Jobs** and open a completed job.
2.  Click **Create Invoice**.
3.  Add parts used (this deducts them from your truck inventory automatically).
4.  Add labor hours.
5.  Click **Review & Charge**.
6.  Enter the customer's credit card details or select a saved card.
7.  Click **Process Payment**.
    *   The money is sent to your Stripe account.
    *   A receipt is automatically emailed to the customer.

### 5. Managing Inventory
1.  Go to **Inventory**.
2.  Here you can see what is on every truck.
3.  To add stock: Click **Restock**, select the truck, and enter the quantities.
4.  **Alert:** You will receive a notification if a specific part drops below the "Reorder Level" you set.
